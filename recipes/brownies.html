Brownies

Description

Craving for a chocolate fix? We got you covered!

Crispy on the outside, melted chocolatey goodness within- this recipe is perfect for weekend baking rituals, or your staple potluck to share with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 250g butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla essence
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup baking chocolate chunks
  • *Optional- a sprinkle of Walnut halves

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter on low heat.
  2. Once melted, remove from heat and mix in sugar until combined. Set aside to cool.
  3. Once cooled, mix in eggs and vanilla essence.
  4. *Optional for next step: Sift in the dry ingredients below (this will make it easier to prevent flour pockets for a uniform batter).
  5. Using a spatula, fold in the flour, cocoa powder and salt. Do your best to ensure no flour pockets remain.
  6. Add in chocolate chunks and combine.
  7. *Optional: Mix in walnuts.
  8. Pour batter into a square baking tray (lined with baking paper).
  9. Bake at 175 degrees Celcius for 35-40 minutes.
